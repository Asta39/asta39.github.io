What is Online Article Writing?

Online article writing involves creating written content for websites, blogs, online publications, and businesses. This can range from short blog posts and product descriptions to in-depth articles, guides, and website copy. It's a versatile hustle that allows you to explore various topics and writing styles. Many Kenyan students find this a flexible way to earn as it can often be done remotely with just a computer and internet access.

It's suitable for individuals who have a good command of English (or other required languages), enjoy research, can meet deadlines, and are able to adapt their writing to different audiences and requirements.

Tools You'll Need

  • A reliable Laptop or Desktop Computer
  • Stable Internet Connection (Fiber, reliable Mobile Data)
  • Word Processing Software (e.g., Microsoft Word, Google Docs)
  • Grammar & Plagiarism Checker (e.g., Grammarly, Copyscape)
  • Optional: Note-taking apps, research tools.

How to Start in Kenya

Starting as an online article writer in Kenya is quite accessible. First, hone your writing skills â€“ practice by starting your own blog or contributing to forums. Create a portfolio of your best work; this could include samples you've written specifically for your portfolio.

Sign up on international freelancing platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, or Freelancer.com. Many of these platforms allow you to receive payments via PayPal, which can then be withdrawn to your M-PESA account. Also, explore local opportunities: some Kenyan businesses and blogs hire freelance writers. Network on LinkedIn or join local Facebook groups for writers and freelancers to find leads.

Be prepared to start with lower rates as you build your reputation and portfolio. Consistency and quality work are key to getting repeat clients and better-paying projects.

Tips for Success

  • Specialize: Consider focusing on a niche you're knowledgeable or passionate about (e.g., technology, finance, travel). This can help you attract specific clients.
  • Build a Strong Portfolio: Showcase your best work. If you're starting, create sample articles on topics you're interested in.
  • Communicate Clearly: Understand client requirements thoroughly and maintain good communication throughout a project.
  • Meet Deadlines: Reliability is crucial for building trust and getting more work.
  • Ask for Testimonials: Positive feedback from clients can be very powerful for attracting new ones.
  • Stay Updated: Learn about SEO basics and current content marketing trends to add more value to your services.
